## Decision Framework

When making decisions, prioritize:
1. Long-term value over short-term gains
2. Learning opportunity over comfort
3. Optionality over commitment
4. Quality over speed (unless urgent)

## Time Philosophy

- Deep work in morning
- Calls/meetings afternoon
- Personal time evening
- No work on weekends (ideally)

## Learning Philosophy

- Learn by doing
- Theory supports practice
- Share knowledge openly
- Document everything

## AI Philosophy

> "AI should augment, not replace human judgment"

- Use AI for scale, not for critical decisions
- Human-in-the-loop for anything high-stakes
- Treat AI as capable assistant, not oracle
- Always verify AI output for important matters
